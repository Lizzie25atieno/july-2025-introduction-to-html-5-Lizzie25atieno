Gazing towards a horizon where she could forge her new self, she departed for Cozy and silent.

She was a muse and the world was her canvas.

Yearning to validate herself,
Oprah's fire kept her Celestial fire Burning.
But whispers aroused, "who would mend the cracks, when the ground give way and the spirit lacks?"
Sharp and laser focused on what suffices her was the goal,
Yet, the cost of ambition, a heavy tide
Gutsy! Dauntless! Resilient! Tenacious! Persistent!
All describe her specialty,
She was symphonic with life

Victory could be her last name.

"In quiet moments, listen and pray"

Hail be thy queen!!


Strong, Powerful, Beautiful, Resilient
Owner of the crown has risen again
Reclaiming her power, she is Reborn!!
